GOAL: Try to defeat all 3 opponents without running out of HP.

GAME INSTRUCTIONS:

Choose a character.

Choose first opponent.

Click "Attack" button to attack.

When you attack, the following happens...

1. Your attack's damage is substracted from the active opponent's Health Power (HP).

2. Opponent counter attacks and your HP is lowered.

3. You "level up" and your damage upon attack increases by 6.

Unlike yours, opponents' damage does not increase each attack.

Once an opponent is defeated, click another to begin the next round.

You DO NOT recover.

If on the welcome screen, click on this instructions box to begin the game.

If you pulled this up using the "Show Instructions" button, re-press the button to hide the instructions.
